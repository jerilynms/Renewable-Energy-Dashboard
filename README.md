# Renewable Energy Dashboard

The data for this dashboard consists of energy consuming sectors, types of renewable energy, consumption in trillions BTU, and year groupings for the timeframe 1973 to 2025. The data was obtained from Kaggle by a user that explained their data cleaning process thoroughly. I reviewed the data for any inconsistencies and found their data very well organized and free of errors.

This dashboard can answer several questions about energy consumption. The first graph on the left is total consumption over time, by sector. We can see that the electric power and transportation sectors increased the most from where they started, with industrial and commercial increasing more modestly. The residential sector appears to have decreased overall consumption. 

Using the filter menu and the two charts on the right, we can analyze each sector in more detail. For example, the residential sector's column chart shows us that we were correct in seeing that energy consumption overall decreases, although it increased after a dip in the 1990s and early 2000s. We can also see that solar and geothermal energy is becoming a larger share of residential energy consumption over time. 

The transportation sector increased energy consumption drastically over the timeframe, but we can see it is completely from biomass fuels. The industrial sector also has a similar history of energy consumption with biomass fuels. 

The sector with the most variation is electric power. Convential hydroelectric power has made up 56.5% of their total consumption over the timeframe; however, we can see that in the last 17 years, wind, solar, and biomasss fuels are beginning to split that consumption more evenly. 

Overall this dashboard could be used by anyone who would like to know more about renewable energy consumption in the last 50 years. For example, which sector is consuming the most renewable energy? If we want to increase the use of solar or wind, which sectors either haven't started utilizing it yet or which ones are already increasing their usage of it? What is the utilization rate of each type of energy by a certain sector? And several other insights as needed. 

Data obtained from Kaggle: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/salehireza2083/renewable-energy-consumption-usa-1973-2025
